What is Nulcast?

Nulcast is a disruptive project by NADSTAT aimed at removing ads from your life. We buy up ad space—billboards, digital banners, bus stops—and leave it blank or with clean, thought-provoking text. It’s not art. It’s not activism. It’s clarity.

Why We're Doing It

Advertising is not neutral. It manipulates behavior, fuels overconsumption, and pollutes mental space. With Nulcast, we’re creating moments of silence in a world that screams for your attention.

Studies on Advertising Harm

  • APA – Chronic Stress & Media Overload
  • NIH – Ad Exposure & Mental Health
  • Frontiers in Psychology – Ads and Body Image
  • Taylor & Francis – Cognitive Load from Outdoor Ads
