What Is Nulcast?

Nulcast is a movement to eliminate advertising from everyday life. We purchase ad space—billboards, signs, online banners—and leave them blank or display simple white text on a black background: “There are no ads here.”

Owned by NADSTAT, a company dedicated to disrupting traditional advertising and reclaiming public attention, Nulcast is our frontline project in this fight.

Why We’re Doing This

Countless studies show advertising contributes to stress, overconsumption, body image issues, and digital addiction. By removing ads, we aim to give people space—mental and physical—to breathe, reflect, and reclaim autonomy.

This is not anti-business. It’s pro-human. Want proof? Scroll down for the research.
