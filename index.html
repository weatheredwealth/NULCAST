Nulcast

Erasing the noise — one blank billboard at a time.

Our Mission

Nulcast, a NADSTAT initiative, is reclaiming public space from visual pollution by buying ad space and leaving it intentionally blank. Every empty billboard is a quiet protest against manipulation and over-commercialization. No logos. No slogans. Just space.

Why It Matters

Studies show that constant advertising can negatively impact mental health, increase anxiety, and reduce attention spans. We aim to bring peace back into our environments — with your help.
