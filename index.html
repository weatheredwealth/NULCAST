Nulcast

Removing ads from public space. Permanently.

What Is Nulcast?

Nulcast is a project by NADSTAT to reclaim public attention. We use donations to purchase advertising space—then leave it empty.

Billboards. Bus stops. Online banners. Instead of noise, we leave behind clarity.

Why We're Doing It

Advertising overload contributes to stress, anxiety, and constant distraction. By removing ads, we return mental and physical space to the public.

This is not anti-business—it's pro-human.
